Meet Dr. Oluremi Adewale

Meet Dr. Oluremi (Remi) Adewale I am a health care practitioner with over twenty years of experience in Canada; a leader; a community advocate. I am a registered Liberal in good standing, a donor and volunteer. I believe in bringing women’s perspectives to the decision-making table so that women have a voice in the planning and promoting activities that can impact their lives and communities. I have put forward my application to run for the position of Organization Chair on the National Women Liberal Commission (NWLC) and I am seeking to earn your support to be the successful candidate.
For me to secure this position, I must receive signatures from at least 25 registered Liberal Women supporters. 

I am a dedicated Liberal; I have integrity and I can do the job. I joined the NWLC as the Organizational Chair in June 2022 and have been a valuable asset to the Commission. I have engaged and contributed in important policy conversations and reviews. I have also attended all the NWLC meetings and contributed positively in discussions to move the commission’s agenda forward. I am very passionate and willing to continue what I have started to support NWLC mandates, to promote the interests of women within the party and to help elect more women as Liberal Members of Parliament.

As a proud Canadian woman of African heritage, I strongly believe in diversity and inclusion. If granted this opportunity, I want to continue to l work with Commission Section Organization Chairs, party leaders, and members to assist in organization responsibilities while promoting diversity of women and the interests of women within the party to help elect female candidates to the House of Commons.

We look forward to you joining us in-person in Ottawa for the 2023 National Convention from May 4th to May 6th, 2023.
